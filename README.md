About This App
JJ's Project Planner is a personal research and writing management tool built to help you stay organized across all the moving parts of a long-form project. Everything is saved locally in your browser — nothing is sent to any server except the optional Email Connector check-in. Use the Import / Export pane to back up your data regularly.

Pane Guide
Session Log
Your primary daily log. Each time you sit down to work, record what you accomplished, how many hours you spent, and what you plan to tackle next session. The previous session's entries are displayed at the top as a reminder when you return. This data flows into the calendar and the Email Connector.

Calendar
View your work history and planning across three scales. The Week view shows accomplishments, hours worked, goals, and deadlines day by day — click any day to edit it. The Month view shows hours worked per day with deadline indicators; click any day to jump to its week. The Year view shows mini monthly calendars with work and deadline highlights; click any month to jump to it. Word-count targets for the visible period are shown at the bottom when a final deadline and word count goal are set.

Deadlines
Track all your project deadlines in one place. Set your Final Project Deadline separately — it's used throughout the app for word-count calculations and is highlighted specially in the calendar. Add other deadlines with a description and date, then mark them complete or incomplete as you go. Incomplete deadlines appear red; completed ones appear green.

Word Count
Enter your total word count goal and your current word count to track writing progress. Once you've set a Final Project Deadline, the app calculates how many words per day you need to write to finish on time. This daily rate is multiplied across whichever calendar view you're looking at — 7 days for the week view, the full month for month view — so you always know your target for the period in front of you.

Email Connector
Sends an automated check-in email to any recipient you choose using your own Gmail account. The email summarizes what you accomplished last session and what you plan to do next. Set a recipient address and your Gmail credentials once — a step-by-step guide is included in the pane. Toggle Auto-send on close to have the email fire automatically whenever you close the app, or send manually using the Send Now button. Credentials are stored encrypted with a password you set.

Meetings
A log of supervisor or advisor meetings. Record the date, who attended, and discussion notes. Add individual action items to each meeting and check them off as you complete them — the collapsed card header shows a running count of completed vs. total actions so you can see at a glance what still needs doing. Meetings are sorted newest-first.

Themes
Choose from fourteen visual themes ranging from clean and minimal to historically inspired. Your selection is saved automatically and restored each time you open the app. Themes change colors, fonts, and the overall mood of the interface.

Import / Export
Back up and restore all your data. Export downloads a timestamped JSON file containing everything — sessions, calendar entries, deadlines, meetings, word count, and settings. Import restores from one of these files, replacing all current data. Export regularly, especially before switching browsers or devices.

Sidebar
Always visible on the right. Shows your next five upcoming deadlines, the final deadline countdown, word count progress, a quick goal-adding tool for any future calendar date, and the Email Connector status.
